Description:
This archive contains reproducibility artifacts for the manuscript “Safety-Gated Edge-AI Compression for Bandwidth-Limited CubeSat EPS Telemetry,” submitted to IEEE Transactions on Aerospace and Electronic Systems. It includes result tables, plotting scripts, firmware profiling artifacts, hardware logs, and reproducibility manifests supporting safety-gated evaluation of CubeSat Electrical Power System telemetry compression and cross-platform embedded profiling on Raspberry Pi 4, ESP32, and STM32 boards.
